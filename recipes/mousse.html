Grandma's Chocolate Mousse

Description

This recipe is Grandma's special chocolate mousse. This recipe is always made at Christmas!

Ingredients

  • 6 eggs
  • 6oz Hersey's Special Dark Chocolate Chips
  • 3 TBSP Creme de Cocoa

Steps

  1. Boil water in the bottom of a double brolier, reducte to medium heat once boiling.
  2. Separate yolks and whites of six eggs.
  3. Add top to double broiler over hot, but not boiling water.
  4. Add chocolate chips and creme de cocoa to top of double broiler. Stir until melted.
  5. Add egg yolks to top of double broiler one at a time, and stir until smooth.
  6. Remove from heat.
  7. Beat six egg whites until stiff.
  8. Fold egg whites completely into chocolate mixture.
  9. Pour into individual serving glass - recommend 4-5oz servings.
  10. Chill. Recommended to make the day before serving.
  11. Top with whipped cream, if desired.
