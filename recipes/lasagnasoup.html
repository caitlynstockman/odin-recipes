Lasagna Soup

Description

This recipe is for cozy, comforting lasagna soup.

Ingredients

Soup
  • 1 TBSP olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, finely diced
  • 1 pound group turkey
  • 5 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 TSP red pepper flakes
  • 1 TSP dried oregano
  • 2 TBSP tomato paste
  • 25oz tomato basil pasta sauce
  • 28oz diced canned tomatoes
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 2 TBSP salt
  • 12 lasagna noodles, uncooked and broken into pieces
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream, optional
Garnishes
  • 1 cup shredded mozzerella cheese
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup ricotta cheese
  • 6 TBSP fresh basil, finely chopped

Steps

Soup
  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot or dutch oven. Add the onion and cook until it's soft.
  2. Add group turkey and saute. Once browned all over, stir in garlic, red pepper flakes, salt, oregano, and tomato paste.
  3. Stir in tomato sauce, diced canned tomatoes, and chicken broth. Brint to boil.
  4. Add broken lasagna noodles, then reduce heat and let simmer unitl noodles are tender.
  5. Stir in heavy cream.
  6. Spoon soup into bowls and server. Top with garnishes, if desired.
Garnishes
  1. Combine mozzerella, parmesan, ricotta, and basil
  2. Top soup with garnishes.
