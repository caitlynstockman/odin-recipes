Small Batch Brownies

Description

This is a small batch brownie recipe.

Ingredients

  • 4 TBSP Butter
  • 1/2 Cup + 1 TBSP Sugar
  • 1/4 Cup + 2 TBSP Cocoa Powder
  • 1 Egg
  • 1 TSP Vanilla
  • 1/4 Cup Flour

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 325F
  2. Melt butter in microwave for 30 seconds, stir, melt for another 30 seconds. Repeat until fully melted.
  3. Add sugar and cocoa powder to melted butter and stir until smooth
  4. Add egg and vanilla to mixture, stir until combined
  5. Stir in flour
  6. Pour batter into a greased pan
  7. Bake for 20-25 minutes
